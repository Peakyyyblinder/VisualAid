HARDWARE COMPONENTS

A breadboard, solderless bread-board or protoboard is a construct-ion base used to build semi proto-types of electronic circuits.

Ultrasonic Sensor ultrasonic sensors are devices that generate or sense ultrasound energy. A transducer to emit and detect object.

Led(Light Emitting Diode) is an electronic device, which emits light when the current passes through its terminals.

Buzzer used for generating sound. It is a digital component that can be connected to digital outputs, and emits a tone when the output is HIGH.

Powerbank provide electrical power to Arduino microcontroller and other electronic devices when not connected to a conventional power source.

Jumper wires are used for making connections between items on breadboard and Arduino's header pins. facilitating the integration.

VISUAL AID SYSTEM

The main goal of this project is to develop a hardware device made especially for the visually impaired community. Its conception, design, and development span several stages. With the combination of sensors, Arduino, and an easy-to-use interface, Peaky Blinder Vision Aid aims to completely transform how visually impaired people move around. By providing users with turn-by-turn guidance, obstacle detection, and real-time audio feedback, this all-inclusive system aims to make walking worry-free.
